About Me

I have spent the past four years as an educator, but I have had an affinity for computers and an interest in how websites work. After much consideration and self study, I eventually decided to attend a coding bootcamp in hopes of changing careers. Since attending George Washington's Fullstack Web Developer Coding Bootcamp, I have fallen in love with programming. There are so many possibilities and many different solutions to the same problem; this fascinates me and I continue to grow as a developer everyday. I have truly found my passion.
